🛡️ Aegis AI
Dynamic Group Insurance Underwriting Platform

Domain: Insurtech | B2B Enterprise SaaS | Healthcare AI

📌 Overview

Traditional group health insurance underwriting relies on static, historical claims data, leading to inaccurate risk assessment and inefficient premium pricing.

Aegis AI transforms this outdated model into a proactive, data-driven underwriting system. By leveraging real-time wearable telemetry and clinical data, the platform generates dynamic Health Risk Scores that enable precise premium pricing and actionable workforce insights.

🚀 Executive Summary

Aegis AI is a B2B SaaS platform designed to modernize insurance underwriting through machine learning and continuous data ingestion.

It enables:

📊 Insurance Providers to improve loss ratio predictions and pricing accuracy
🏢 Corporate HR Teams to validate and optimize wellness investments
🔐 Secure Data Pipelines to convert health data into financial risk signals
❗ Problem Statement
1. Insurance Challenge

Underwriters lack real-time health insights, making it difficult to accurately predict:

Probability of claims
Expected loss ratios
Optimal premium pricing
2. Corporate HR Challenge

Organizations invest in wellness programs but cannot:

Prove measurable ROI
Demonstrate reduced health risks to insurers
Negotiate better premiums
3. Technology Gap

There is no unified infrastructure to:

Normalize fragmented wearable and clinical data
Translate health metrics into underwriting intelligence
💡 Solution

Aegis AI introduces a three-tier intelligent underwriting architecture:

🧩 Tier 1: Data Ingestion & Normalization (API Layer)
Secure integrations with wearable platforms (e.g., Apple HealthKit, Google Fit – simulated)
Clinical data ingestion from EHR systems
Data standardization across:
Daily steps
Resting heart rate
Sleep patterns
Clinical events
🤖 Tier 2: AI Underwriting Engine (Core ML)
Machine Learning models (e.g., XGBoost, Random Forest)
Predicts:
Health Risk Score
Expected Loss Ratio
Applies credit-risk modeling logic to insurance underwriting
Enables dynamic premium adjustments
📊 Tier 3: B2B Client Success Portal (Dashboard UI)
Role-based secure access for:
Insurance Underwriters
Corporate HR Teams
Features:
Workforce health analytics
Predictive premium modeling
Wellness program ROI tracking
