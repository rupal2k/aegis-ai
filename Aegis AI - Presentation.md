## End-to-end intelligence pipeline

<div style="font-size:0.84em;margin-top:0.4rem;">

| Step | Input | Output |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Ingest** | HRMS, wearables, clinical, claims data | Unified employee health dataset |
| **Score** | XGBoost on 21 health features per employee | Health Risk Score (HRS) 0–100 |
| **Explain** | SHAP analysis on every prediction | Top 5 risk drivers — fully traceable |
| **Price** | HRS + zone + industry multipliers | Risk-adjusted premium recommendation |
| **Report** | Dashboard views + PDF export | Wellness ROI playbook + underwriting decision |

</div>

<br>

> From raw workforce data to underwriting decision — in under a second.
