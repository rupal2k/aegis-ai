# Gift Cards & Rewards

Reference for managing points, gift cards, milestone prizes, and reward fulfillment.

---

## Reward Layers On Vantage Fit

| Reward type | Description | Typical use |
|---|---|---|
| Badges | Digital recognition only | Milestone or participation recognition |
| Points | Platform reward currency | Ongoing incentive and redemption |
| Gift cards | Real-value rewards | Completion or winner rewards |
| Custom prizes | Client-provided rewards | Internal merch, extra PTO, special recognition |
| Leaderboard prizes | Rank-based rewards | Top performers or top teams |

---

## Marketplace Context

The broader Vantage Fit reward story includes point-based redemption and gift-card style rewards. Common examples referenced in internal product notes include brands such as Amazon, Starbucks, and Nike.

**Do not promise a specific vendor or catalog item until availability is confirmed for that client, region, and program setup.**

---

## Before Challenge Setup

- [ ] Confirm whether rewards are part of the challenge
- [ ] Confirm reward type: badge, points, gift card, or custom prize
- [ ] Confirm budget and approval owner
- [ ] Confirm whether fulfillment is platform-based, manual, or client-managed
- [ ] Write the reward structure into the challenge notes

---

## During Challenge

- [ ] Track milestone logic clearly
- [ ] Do not distribute rewards before the agreed milestone or challenge close
- [ ] QC eligibility before anything is issued

---

## After Challenge Ends

- [ ] Export the eligible participant list
- [ ] Cross-check against milestone or leaderboard results
- [ ] Get client confirmation if required
- [ ] Coordinate fulfillment
- [ ] Log completion in CRM or account notes

---

## Fulfillment Checklist

- [ ] Eligibility list verified
- [ ] Client approval received
- [ ] Value and reward type confirmed
- [ ] Delivery method confirmed
- [ ] Fulfillment completed
- [ ] Any failed delivery cases resolved
- [ ] Final confirmation logged

---

## Common Issues

| Issue | Action |
|---|---|
| Participant disputes eligibility | Pull milestone data and verify rules used |
| Reward not received | Confirm email, delivery method, and platform status |
| Budget overage risk | Pause and get approval before distribution |
| Client wants to change rewards mid-challenge | Confirm feasibility, update notes, and make sure communication is handled |

---

## Communication Rules

- Make rewards explicit in the challenge description before launch
- Never promise a reward to an individual employee outside the approved client plan
- Route reward disputes through the client admin whenever possible
- Keep a written record of every reward commitment
