## Key SOPs

### Client Onboarding Flow
1. Receive client confirmation
2. Create company instance
3. Configure platform access
4. Confirm reporting owners, notifications, and reward setup
5. Send credentials and welcome email
6. Configure sample challenge
7. Confirm employee upload process and device-sync guidance
8. Schedule onboarding walkthrough
9. Monitor first-week adoption

### Challenge Setup Flow
1. Understand challenge goal
2. Confirm duration and audience
3. Pick the right challenge engine and metric mix
4. Define milestones and rewards
5. Configure challenge
6. Internal QC check
7. Client approval
8. Launch and monitor

### Invoice Review Process
1. Validate active users for the billing cycle
2. Match agreement and pricing terms
3. Verify discounts or overage rules
4. Coordinate with data team
5. Review discrepancies
6. Confirm before invoice raise

## High-Value Reminders

- Lead with adoption, not feature lists.
- Keep first launches simple. Prove value fast.
- Never quote unverified client metrics.
- Tata Motors and Wipro each have multiple distinct programs. Label them correctly.
- Use aggregated reporting for HR wherever possible.
