# Approved Proof Points & Buyer Messaging

Quick reference for client calls, renewal conversations, and written communication.

---

## Core Message

**Vantage Fit is a wellness platform employees love to use.**

That message works best when it is supported by:

- native mobile adoption
- challenges and gamification
- real rewards
- measurable engagement data

---

## Speak To The Buyer

| Buyer | What they care about | Best angle |
|---|---|---|
| HR Director / Benefits lead | Adoption, engagement, admin ease | "A platform employees actually use" |
| CHRO / leadership | Culture, retention, visible outcomes | "Wellness engagement you can show to leadership" |
| CFO / commercial reviewer | Cost, ROI, predictable billing | "Measurable usage and clear reporting" |
| Admin / IT contact | Low overhead, setup clarity, privacy | "Simple rollout, controlled access, aggregated reporting" |

---

## Feature-To-Value Translation

| Product feature | How to say it in client language |
|---|---|
| Native mobile apps | Drives adoption without employees feeling pushed into another unused tool |
| Leaderboards and challenges | Friendly competition keeps participation moving |
| Points and marketplace rewards | Real rewards help participation stay high over time |
| Reports and analytics | HR can see engagement trends and share progress internally |
| Wearable integration | Works with devices employees already own |
| Wellness score and broader wellness features | Goes beyond step counting into fuller wellbeing support |

## Data Accuracy Rules

- Never round up. `59%` stays `59%`.
- Tata Motors has two distinct programs. Do not mix their stats.
- Wipro has three separate challenge programs in the 2025 story. Label single-program stats clearly.
- IBS Software was a 28-day challenge, not 30 days.
- Landmark Group is not the same as Landmark Leisure.
- POSOCO is not POSCO.
- If a metric is not confirmed, do not improvise. Ask for the source or use qualitative language instead.

## Easy Messaging Formula

Use this pattern when you need to explain value quickly:

**Client problem -> Vantage Fit capability -> measurable outcome -> proof point**

Example:

Low adoption -> native mobile app plus challenges and rewards -> stronger engagement -> IBS Software reached 88% engagement in 28 days.
