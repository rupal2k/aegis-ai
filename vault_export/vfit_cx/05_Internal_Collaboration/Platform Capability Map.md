# Platform Capability Map

Quick internal map for answering "Can Vantage Fit do this?" and routing the next step well.

---

## Capability Overview

| Area | Available capability | What CX can confidently say |
|---|---|---|
| Activity tracking | Steps, active minutes, manual activity logging | Employees can participate through daily movement and logged activity |
| Connected devices | Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin | The platform supports common wearable-sync use cases |
| Challenges | Race, streak, journey, level-up, marathon, multi-activity, training, templates | The platform can support both simple launches and richer challenge programs |
| Gamification | Leaderboards, badges, points, leagues | Participation can be reinforced through visible progress and rewards |
| Wellness features | Wellness score, HRA, vitals/goals, mindfulness | The product goes beyond steps into broader wellbeing |
| Nutrition | Food logging and calorie tracking | Clients can run more complete habit-based programs |
| Community | Teams, groups, chats, events | Wellness can be social and team-oriented |
| Rewards | Points plus marketplace-style redemption | Real incentives can be part of the engagement strategy |
| Admin platform | Dashboard, reports, notifications, user management, surveys | HR gets visibility and control, not just an employee app |

---

## Best Internal Routing

| Need | Primary team |
|---|---|
| Active-user count or reporting pull | Data team |
| Bug, sync issue, broken feature | Product or backend |
| New feature request | Product |
| Flyer, banner, campaign visual | Marketing |
| Design polish or branded visual asset | Design |
| Commercial exception or billing approval | Manager or finance owner |

---

## Common Client Questions And Good CX Framing

| Client asks | Good framing |
|---|---|
| "Is this only for step challenges?" | No. Vantage Fit supports broader wellness programs, including mindfulness, nutrition, and multi-activity challenge formats. |
| "Will employees actually use it?" | The strongest Vantage Fit story is adoption: native mobile use, gamification, and rewards. |
| "Can we show leadership the impact?" | Yes. Reporting and analytics are part of the admin-side value story. |
| "Do employees need special devices?" | No. Device support helps, but the value story should not depend on new hardware. |

---

## CX Guardrails

- Promise capabilities, not custom engineering timelines
- Confirm configuration details before speaking in absolutes
- Use approved proof points when talking outcomes
- Pull data when the conversation moves from possibility to measurement
