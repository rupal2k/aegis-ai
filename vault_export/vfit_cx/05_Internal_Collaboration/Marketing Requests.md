# Marketing Requests

Process and templates for requesting marketing support for client-facing materials.

---

## When To Involve Marketing

| Need | Involve marketing? |
|---|---|
| Challenge flyer for client | Yes |
| Welcome creative for new client | Yes |
| Promotional campaign for challenge launch | Yes |
| Email banner or campaign visual | Yes |
| Standard email template | No |
| Internal-only notes or SOPs | No |

---

## Typical Request Types

| Request type | Description | Typical lead time |
|---|---|---|
| Challenge flyer | Branded launch poster or visual | 3 to 5 business days |
| Welcome creative | Onboarding visual or launch kit | 3 to 5 business days |
| Email banner | Header visual for client comms | 2 to 3 business days |
| Social post | Internal or external campaign asset | 5 to 7 business days |
| Full promo campaign | Multi-asset support for a large launch | 2 or more weeks |

---

## What To Include In The Brief

- client name
- deliverable type
- campaign or challenge name
- dates
- CTA
- deadline
- client brand needs
- source logo or approved client assets

Use [[07_Templates/Challenge Flyers Brief]] as the base template.

---

## Brand Quick Rules For Requests

If the request is Vantage Fit branded or co-branded, remember:

- Core message: `A wellness platform employees love to use`
- Primary brand color: `#F15162`
- Dark background: `#1D2228`
- Font family: Poppins
- Preferred imagery: everyday wellness, walking, yoga, cycling, healthy routines
- Avoid heavy gym or bodybuilder-style visuals

If you want proof-point language on the creative, use only approved metrics from [[03_Client_Communication/Approved Proof Points & Buyer Messaging]].

---

## Review Before Sending To Client

- check challenge name
- check dates
- check CTA
- check brand spelling and logo usage
- check proof points and statistics
- check whether the asset matches the client tone and audience
