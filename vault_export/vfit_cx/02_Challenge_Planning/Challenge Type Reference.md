# Challenge Type Reference

Quick reference for picking the right Vantage Fit challenge design.

---

## Core Challenge Engines

| Type | Best use case | Strong metrics/themes | Typical duration | Watch-outs |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Race / competition | High-energy launch, leaderboard focus | Steps, distance, calories | 2 to 6 weeks | Can feel repetitive if overused |
| Streak | Habit building and consistency | Hydration, mindfulness, reading, daily logging | 7 to 30 days | Needs clear daily rules |
| Journey / route-based | Narrative engagement and shared momentum | Steps or distance | 2 to 8 weeks | Distance target must be realistic |
| Level-up | Progressive improvement | Steps, minutes, mixed targets | 3 to 6 weeks | Complexity rises if goals change too fast |
| E-marathon | Endurance campaigns | Distance, run/walk activity | 2 to 8 weeks | Best for already motivated groups |
| Multi-activity | Whole-person wellness | Steps plus hydration plus mindfulness | 2 to 6 weeks | Needs simple scoring explanation |
| Training | Coaching-style behavior change | Workouts, running plans, guided activity | 2 to 8 weeks | Requires stronger participant commitment |
| Templated | Fast onboarding and repeatable launches | Starter challenge formats | 1 to 4 weeks | Still needs client-specific QC |

---

## Theme vs. Engine

One easy way to avoid setup confusion:

- **Engine** = the challenge structure in the platform
- **Theme or metric** = what employees are actually doing inside it

Examples:

- A hydration campaign is often a **streak** challenge
- A mindfulness push can be a **streak** or **multi-activity** challenge
- A city-to-city walk is usually a **journey / route-based** challenge
- A first-launch "simple steps challenge" is often a **race / competition**

---

## Good First-Challenge Matches

| Client situation | Recommended starting point |
|---|---|
| Brand-new client with low platform familiarity | Templated steps challenge |
| Team culture focus | Team race or route-based challenge |
| Mental health awareness month | Mindfulness streak |
| Wellness week or short campaign | 7-day hydration or habit streak |
| Client wants broad wellness participation | Multi-activity challenge |

---

## Reward Pairing Guide

| Challenge type | Reward style that usually works well |
|---|---|
| Race / competition | Leaderboard prizes, points, badges |
| Streak | Completion badges, milestone points |
| Journey / route-based | Checkpoint badges, finish-line reward |
| Level-up | Tiered milestone rewards |
| Multi-activity | Broad participation rewards |
| Training | Completion recognition and light milestone rewards |
